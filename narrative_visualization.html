Life Expectancy versus Health Expenditure per Capita

Health Expenditure:

The above narrative visualization delineates the relationship between Life Expectancy at birth in years versus Health Expenditure per Capita in current US$ for select countries for the years 2001, 2010, and 2019. Points may be examined in more detail by hovering the pointer over a point of interest, which displays an annotation with details about the datapoint.

Data was sourced from the World Bank World Development Indicators dataset.
